## Full Content

Studio Kiln’s response centres on a deceptively simple insight: if Fellowkids builds fandom for others, their brand should behave like a fan itself. “In a culture that spots posturing instantly, anything that read like a corporate cosplay would die on arrival,” Co-Founder & Creative Director Nathan Smith explains. “Our response to the brief was to design an identity that doesn’t talk at fans but behaves like one – responsive, playful and able to move with the community.”

That thinking led to the Fellowkid character – a hooded figure that became the identity’s emotional core. The choice wasn’t arbitrary. Across esports, entertainment and sporting communities, the hoodie functions as a universal signal, instantly recognisable in arenas and streams. “If our answer to the brief was to build a brand that behaves like a fan, then we should put the fan at the heart of the brand: the Fellowkid,” Smith notes.
